Our Courses
Active courses focus on developing job skills & prepare students to become job-ready in a short span of time. These courses involve in-depth study & application of concepts using the latest technology. Our diploma course builds the foundation for a career in Software Engineering, Animation, Web Designing, Multimedia or Finance Management etc.
All our courses have been designed to provide flexible learning to the students. Learn various software and Languages like Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere, Autodesk 3D Max, Autodesk Maya, Tally 9.0 ERP, Adobe Dreamweaver, C/C++, Java, .Net, etc.
We pay extra emphasis to the fact that training curriculums require constant and ongoing development which is founded upon long hours of research and interaction with the industry. This coupled with an integrated instructor development and training program which ensures that our highly talented teams of instructors are always kept at the helm of technology and are equipped with the finest techno-creative skills translates into the next generation of highly talented students who are charged up to handle the most challenging work scenarios right from day one.

List of Courses

Software Engineering
This program builds a strong foundation in IT, software engineering principles, enterprise development frameworks, project management and much more. This course focuses on developing skills in .NET and J2EE Technologies.

Web Designing
We offers you the best courses to all who want to be professional of web designing. The web designing courses offered by us are designed keeping in mind the present requirements of the internet solution industry.

Graphic Designing
This unique program is the reason why Active students have an outstanding employment record locally and nationally in diverse fields such as in Design Studios, Publishing Houses, TV, Post Production Houses, Ad Agencies and News Media Studios etc.

Financial Management
This Program prepares you for an accounting job. This will include working on accounts, book keeping, taxation and the accounting software Tally ERP9. Apart from this there will be extra modules on communication skills and personality development to ensure you clear your interviews.

Multimedia
Multimedia course trains you in various media applications such as Print, Moving & Interactive media. Make a career in this dynamic industry with this course. Learn all techniques of digital graphics & image editing for print & publishing

Hardware and Networking
This program builds a strong hardware & networking fundamentals, enterprise network solutions management concepts and security solutions management and much more. Active provides an opportunity to persue specialization in Linux or Microsoft platform.

Short Term Courses
Short-term courses are for students & animation professionals who wish to develop skill-sets relevant to their career in a very short span of time.
