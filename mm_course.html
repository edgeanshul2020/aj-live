Advance Diploma in MultiMedia (03-ADM)
ADM is our most comprehensive programme in Animation and Multimedia. This one year course covers complete gamut of Graphic Design, Sound Editing & Video Editing from pre production to 3D production & post production level. We assure students a 100% job placement at the end of their curriculum. One should ideally possess a basic knowledge about handling computers. In the animation industry, aspirants can further specialize as a 3D or 2D modeler, special FX creator, animator, character designer, games designer, and interaction designer.

Everybody loves to see animation and enjoy their effects but this multimedia course at Active Computer, a trusted Computer Institute in Delhi, train you create your own animations and many other multimedia effects anybody ever has thought. Our course in multimedia aimed to bring out the artistic designer in you who has mastered various software packages in multimedia like modeling, animation and many more. Through this course you are ready to surprise the crowd with astonishing animated creations. We offer the same multimedia course in different levels such as ADVANCED, SHORT TERM and SPECIAL as per your requirements.
